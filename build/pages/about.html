About CivicSync

We're building bridges between citizens and local authorities to create stronger, more responsive communities.

Our Mission

CivicSync was born from the simple belief that every citizen's voice matters. We've seen too many civic issues go unreported, untracked, and unresolved due to communication barriers between communities and local authorities.

Our platform transforms how civic engagement works, making it easier for citizens to report issues and for authorities to respond efficiently. We're not just building software â€“ we're fostering transparent, accountable governance.

Our Story

CivicSync started as a university project and evolved into a mission to transform civic engagement through technology.

January 2024

The Idea

Started as a university software engineering project to address the communication gap between citizens and local authorities in urban areas.

March 2024

First Prototype

Developed the first working prototype with basic issue reporting and authority dashboard features after extensive user research.

June 2024

Community Testing

Launched beta testing with 50+ citizens and 5 municipal departments, receiving valuable feedback for platform improvements.

Present

Growing Impact

Today, CivicSync connects thousands of citizens with local authorities, facilitating transparent and efficient civic issue resolution.
